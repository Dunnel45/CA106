U9 up to over 35s

GAA is enjoyed by all and teams are organised satraight from u9 and can be played even in the later stages of life with the wildly popular over 35s leagues spread across Ireland.

County Rivalries

All gaelic supporters like to get behind their counties wholeheartedly, this has lead to numerous notorious rivalries building between different teams such as the Dublin-Kerry Rivalry, a fierce competitiveness between 2 of the best teams in Ireland.

Croke Park

The stadium is one of the largest in Europe, with a capacity of 82,300 and can accommodate all types of events - from field sports to concerts,The largest recorded attendance for Croke Park was a whopping 90,556 for Offaly against Down in the All Ireland Football Final.
