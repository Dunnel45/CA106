Horses

There are many breeds of horses used in horse racing. The of the most common are Arabians, Standardbreds, and American quarter Horses

Jockeys

Horses are rid by jockeys. Some of the best jockeys of all time include Bill Shoemaker, Lester Piggot, and Eddie Arcaro.

Tracks

Horse racing is held on tracks. The most famous tracks are Ascot, Churchill Towns, and Aintree
