Irish history

Ireland has a strong horse racing history and is considered one of the oldest traditions in the country’s history. Horse racing is one of the country’s most popular spectator sport.

Irish bred horses are well known for their amazing stamina and speed Horse racing has been a part of Irish history dating as far back as 60 AD. Records and text from this time give accounts of horse races taking place in Curragh and Galway


Back in these times the Irish highlands were the perfect natural habitat for wild horses and early settlers domesticated these animals to use for things such as: travel, food and leather


Horses were also trained for war. Early Irish settlers soon realized that horse riding was a skill soon made it a competitive sport to find the best riders. These jockeys were the celebrities of their time.
