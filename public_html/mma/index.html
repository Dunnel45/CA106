Organisations

MMA organisations sign fighters and organise events. The biggest organisation in the world is the UFC. It has the highst level fighters and is the most popular. Two other popular organiusations are ONE Championship and Bellator

Arenas

Fights are commonly held in a cage shaped like an octagon.This is due to the popularity of the UFC who were the most popular organisation to use this format and are credited with popularising it.However before this MMA competitions could be found in a boxing ring, square cage, and even an inverted dome.

Fights

There are dozens of different MMA organisations and thousands of fights happen a year. The UFC has had 39 events in 2018 which averages out to about 468 fights not taking injuries into account,Bellator has had 23 events(about 299 fights), and ONE Championship has had 24 events(roughly 288 fights)
