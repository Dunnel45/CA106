Introduction

This section is focused on mixed martial arts(MMA). The sports history will be explained from its historical roots, to its early days as a combat sport, to the present day state of the sport.

What is MMA

Wikipedia defines MMA as “a full-contact combat sport that allows striking and grappling, both standing and on the ground, using techniques from various combat sports and martial arts”. This, while correct is a wordy explanation. MMA is simply two or more martial arts, at least one striking and one grappling, put into practice at the same time. These can be any martial arts but some of the most common are….
