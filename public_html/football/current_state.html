Football today

  • Lifestyle and money

    When football first started it wasn't even considered a career nevermind being paid.
    Players of the game first started being compensated on the 20/July/1885 when the FA legalised pay.
    Nowadays the players earn superfluous money, for example the Argentinian player Lionel Messi earns a salary of 40M EUR.
    In the past footballers would earn the same wage as the fans watching them, nowdays the money the fans earn and the players make is incomparable.
    Up until the 1960s couldn't earn more than 20 pound a week.
    Modern era players are influenced on what club to play with etc. by money however in the past it was just passion for the game.
    Footballers are now treated like celebrities due to the amount of money they are earning, this could be killing the love for the game.

  • Changes in the game

    There has been lots of changes in Football during the years.
    The pace of the modern game has been altered, todays game is much faster than the older styles of football.
    Matches are now more intense, with players sprinting much more than they used to due to a more aggressive style of counter attacking play.
    Techonlogical advances has brought new opportunities into the the world of soccer.
    For example goal line technology is now implemented into the game.
    This determines whether the whole of the ball has crossed the goal line.
    This ensures the referees decision is correct and reduces arguments on whether a goal should stand or not.
