Leagues

There are thousands of football leagues in the world, this includes professional leagues, semi-pro leagues, amateur leagues, youth leagues and more, however the top 5 entertaining professional leagues in the world include the French Ligue 1, Italian Serie A, German Bundesliga, Spanish La Liga and English premier league

Stadiums

There is currently 4,775 know stadiums in over 224 countries, These stadiums rank from large to small capacity, The Rungrado May Stadium in North Korea is the largest stadium in the world, it has a whopping seating capacity of 114,000

Competitions

Football is home to a wide variety of tournaments and competitions, the most popular being the FIFA world cup, this tournament consists of many international teams all over the world, There are lots of other cups and competitions in the world of football such as the UEFA champions league and the FA cup.
