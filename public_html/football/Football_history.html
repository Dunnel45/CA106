The Kick Off

  • Forgotten Football

    Football is an ancient sport and was played in many different forms before it was an official sport.
    The oldest ball game which resembled football goes as far back as the second and third centuries BC in China.
    According to FIFA the variation of the game was called Tsu'Chu and had similar comparisions to football as it was a ball sport which followed related rules.
    This game comprised of kicking a leather ball into a small net made of bamboo.
    Other early games linked with football includes:A prehistoric ancestor of Football which comes from Asia, where players pass the ball to eachother in a small space Episkyros, which is a Greek form of football played with a smaller ball

  • The FA

    It all started in England in the year 1863, when rugby football and association football split apart.
    This partition caused the Football association(FA) in England to be established. The FA was footballs first comittee or administrators.
    With no set of rules disputes were easily caused as clubs had their own version of football.
    The FA was formed so rules could be set in place for the sport.
    The first rules were as follows, The game would consist of dribbling the ball, Players could implement "hacking" which involved kicking opponents shins, The FA grew incredibly quick, eight years after the association had 50 member clubs.
    The FA created a chain reaction which caused the spread of football globally.
